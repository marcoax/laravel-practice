# Lesson 10 — Laravel AI SDK

> Version: 13.0 (Mar 17, 2026) · Type: new · Source: laravel-news.com/laravel-13

## What changed
The AI SDK leaves beta as a **first-party** package, stable from the day Laravel 13
shipped. It offers a provider-agnostic API (OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini…) for: text
generation, agents with tool-calling, embeddings, images, audio. It handles retries,
error normalization and queue integration.

## Why it might matter
It standardizes AI integrations that today are often hand-rolled, and it's a
first-party alternative to community solutions like Prism. If you're thinking about
AI features, start here.
